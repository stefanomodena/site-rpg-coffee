About RPG&Coffee

RPG&Coffee is a coffee shop in Boa Viagem, Recife, created to bring together people who love coffee, culture, games, and good conversation. More than a place for specialty coffee, we are a space to meet friends, make new connections, and enjoy experiences that go far beyond the table.

Here you can play tabletop RPG, explore a wide collection of board games, enjoy video game consoles from many generations, play on our Game Pass–equipped computer, and take part in courses, workshops, tournaments, meetups, and events held regularly. We also offer a comfortable setting for reading, studying, working, or simply relaxing while you enjoy coffee prepared with different brewing methods.

Our menu combines specialty coffees, drinks, sweets, and carefully prepared meals to make every visit even more enjoyable. Whether for a board game session, an RPG campaign, a meetup with friends, or just discovering new flavors, RPG&Coffee was designed to welcome people of all ages in a warm, laid-back atmosphere.

We welcome visitors from Brazil and around the world with service in 🇵🇹 Portuguese, 🇬🇧 English, 🇪🇸 Spanish, and 🇨🇳 Mandarin, so everyone can enjoy the experience with comfort and hospitality.

Games for every generation

At RPG&Coffee in Boa Viagem, Recife, video games are part of the experience. We offer consoles from different generations so you can revisit great classics or discover the latest releases — whether for a quick match with friends or hours of fun.

Our collection includes PlayStation 2, PlayStation 3, PlayStation 4, and PlayStation 5, Xbox 360, Xbox Series S with Game Pass access, Nintendo Switch, Nintendo 64, plus an arcade cabinet and a selection of retro games via emulators, bringing together titles that shaped video game history.

If you love adventures, RPGs, racing, sports, fighting, platformers, local multiplayer, or the classics that defined generations, there will always be something to play. Our goal is to offer a comfortable space to gather friends, meet new players, and share a passion for video games while enjoying specialty coffees, drinks, and our menu.

Whether you want to relive a classic, try a PlayStation exclusive, compete on Xbox, explore Nintendo worlds, or discover new games through Game Pass, RPG&Coffee is a meeting place for video game lovers in Recife.

Our Story

2019 - The Beginning

RPG&Coffee was born from a shared dream among friends passionate about coffee and geek culture.

2021 - First Literary Meetup

2022 - Expansion

2023 - Recognition

2026 - Looking Ahead

We keep expanding our activities, with new games, new products, and new events.
